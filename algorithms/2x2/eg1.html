2x2 EG-1

A last layer algorithm set where the 2 pieces of the solved face must be adjacently swapped.

Where to keep the solved bar?

Mostly, the solved bar should be on the back, only for some cases the bar must be held on left (AKA 'LEG-1'), such cases will have [x'] at the beginning of the alg.
